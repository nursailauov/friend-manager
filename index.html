HOW TO USE
1

Get Your Authentication Token

Choose your login method and paste your token:

  • JWT Token – Direct token from game
  • Access Token – Garena OAuth token
  • EAT Token – External access token
  • UID:Password – Your account credentials

After entering, click AUTHENTICATE to load your profile.

2

Friend Management

Once authenticated, you can:

  • Send Friend Request – Enter target UID and choose "Send Friend Request"
  • Remove Friend – Enter friend's UID and choose "Remove Friend"
  • Accept/Reject – Use the "Pending Requests" tab to handle incoming requests
3

View Lists

Switch between tabs to see your current friends or pending friend requests. Friends display avatar, nickname, UID, region, and level. Pending requests have Accept/Reject buttons.

4

Important Notes

  • Region is auto-detected from your token
  • Session expires after 10 minutes for security
  • Your tokens are never stored on our server
